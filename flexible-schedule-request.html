Instructions: Flexible Work Schedule Request

Important: Read these instructions first to choose the best way to complete this form.

About Electronic Signatures

Typing your name in the signature field is acceptable as your electronic signature. You do not need to add a handwritten signature unless you prefer to do so.

How to Complete This Form

Choose the option that works best for you:

Option 1: Fill Out Online, Then Save as PDF

  1. Complete all fields in your web browser
  2. Type your name in the signature field (this counts as your signature)
  3. Click the Print button (top right corner of the screen)
  4. Select "Save as PDF" as your printer
  5. Save the completed PDF to your computer
  6. Email the PDF to your supervisor for approval

Option 2: Save Blank PDF, Then Fill Digitally

Best for: Those who want to use Adobe Fill & Sign tools

  1. Click the Print button (top right corner)
  2. Select "Save as PDF" as your printer and save the blank form
  3. Open the PDF in Adobe Acrobat Reader or your preferred PDF viewer
  4. Use Fill & Sign tools to complete all fields and add your signature
  5. Save the completed PDF
  6. Email the PDF to your supervisor for approval

Option 3: Print on Paper and Complete by Hand

  1. Click the Print button (top right corner)
  2. Select your printer and print the form on paper
  3. Fill out all fields with a pen
  4. Sign the form
  5. Either:
    • Scan the completed form and email the PDF to your supervisor, or
    • Deliver the physical paper form to your supervisor

What Happens Next

After your supervisor approves and signs the form, they will forward it to the Office of Human Resources (OHR) for final processing.

Need Help?

If you have questions about completing this form, contact your supervisor or the Office of Human Resources.
